Who We Are

A Lean, Focused Federal Contractor

Ventoro Ventures LLC is a registered SAM.gov vendor and Florida-based small business specializing in federal government contracting. We connect agency needs with qualified professionals — delivering on time, within scope, and without friction.

Founded by Robert Brodner and Julian Janin, Ventoro was built on a straightforward principle: the best contractors execute cleanly and communicate clearly.

Small Business Certified
Registered under SBA small business size standards. Eligible for micro-purchase and simplified acquisition awards.
Nationwide Coverage

Direct & Responsive
Every contracting officer speaks directly with a founder. No layers, no delays. Fast responses on every contract.
