Who We Are

A Lean, Focused Florida Contractor

Ventoro Ventures LLC is a registered SAM.gov vendor and Florida-based small business specializing in federal and state government contracting. We connect agency needs with qualified professionals — delivering on time, within scope, and without friction.

Founded by Robert Brodner and Julian Janin, Ventoro was built on a simple principle: the best government contractors execute cleanly and communicate clearly. That's what we do.

Small Business Certified
Registered under SBA small business size standards across all active NAICS codes. Eligible for micro-purchase and simplified acquisition awards.
Florida Based & Nationwide
Headquartered in Jupiter, Florida. We bid on contracts statewide and nationwide, coordinating qualified local professionals wherever the work is performed.
Direct & Responsive
When a contracting officer reaches out, they speak directly with a founder. No layers, no delays. Fast responses and clean documentation on every contract.
