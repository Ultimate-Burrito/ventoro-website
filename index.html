Mission
OUR
PURPOSE

Ventoro Ventures exists to bridge the gap between government agencies and the qualified professionals who serve them. We take on the complexity of contracting so agencies can focus on what they do best — and so the right people get the right work done, on time and without friction.

Who We Are

BUILT TO
EXECUTE.

Ventoro Ventures LLC is a Florida-based small business founded by Robert Brodner and Julian Janin. We are a registered vendor on SAM.gov and MyFloridaMarketplace, specializing in connecting federal and state agency needs with qualified local subcontractors.

We are the middlemen who make it work. We find the contract, source the right professionals, manage the paperwork, and deliver on time. No bureaucracy, no delays — just clean execution.

01
Florida Based
Headquartered in Jupiter, Florida. Registered with the State of Florida and operating across all 50 states to fulfill federal and state contracts anywhere they arise.
02
Small Business Certified
Registered under SBA small business size standards. Eligible for micro-purchase awards, simplified acquisitions, and small business set-aside contracts.
03
Founder Direct
Every contracting officer speaks directly with Robert or Julian. No account managers, no middlemen on our side. Fast responses and clean documentation every time.
