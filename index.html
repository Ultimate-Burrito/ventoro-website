CLASS: CORPORATE PROFILE

Who We Are

Built to execute.
Wired for the government.

Ventoro Ventures LLC is a Florida-based small business founded by Robert Brodner and Julian Janin. We are registered vendors on SAM.gov and MyFloridaMarketplace — positioned to serve federal, state, county, and local government agencies across every service category we operate in.

We are the middlemen who make it work. We win the contract, source the right subcontractors, manage the paperwork, and deliver on time. When a contracting officer reaches out to Ventoro Ventures, they speak directly with a founder — no layers, no delays.

Florida Based

Headquartered in Jupiter, Florida. Registered with the State of Florida and active on MyFloridaMarketplace for state and local contracts.

Small Business Certified

Registered under SBA small business size standards. Eligible for micro-purchase awards, simplified acquisitions, and small business set-asides.

Nationwide Coverage

Based in Florida, operating in all 50 states. We coordinate qualified local professionals wherever the work is performed.
